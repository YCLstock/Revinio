How To Read The Gragh

The left part shows the departments that every applicant was in. Hovering over it will display the full name of the department. Colors represent the choices you made in the filter, and the areas of the color also indicate the percentage of your decision. Choose your preference by clicking on the bar.

The chosen departments will appear on the right. The dots represent the degree of the candidates. By clicking on a dot or selecting the dots, you will be allowed to leave a comment and select or remove. The dots that were selected will be red, and the dots being selected will be pink and appear solid. (You can use the button to only show their highest education)

Job Position

How To Read The Gragh

The left part displays every applicant's work position. Hovering over it will show the full name of the position. Colors represent the choices you made in the filter, and the areas of the color also indicate the percentage of your decision.

Choose your preference by clicking on the bar. The chosen positions will appear on the right. The dots represent the job changing frequency of the candidates. By clicking on a dot or selecting the dots, you will be able to comment and select or remove. The dots that were selected will be red, and the dots being selected will be pink and appear solid. (You can use the button to only show their latest job position)
